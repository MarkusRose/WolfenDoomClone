#####################################
#      WolfenDoomClone              #
#   an FPS for the console          #
#####################################

This game was created from the helpful videos of javidx9 on youtube 
"Code-It-Yourself! First Person Shooter (Quick and Simple C++)"

Very basic, no shooting, just walking and bumping into walls.

Keyboard controls:
W: Forward
S: Backward
A: Rotate left
D: Rotate right
Q: Strafe left
E: Strafe right

Console layout:
Please keep the console at 
width: 120
height: 40
and choose a good looking font.
